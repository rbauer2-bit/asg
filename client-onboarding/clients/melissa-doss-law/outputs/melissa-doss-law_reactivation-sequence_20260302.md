## STRATEGY NOTES
*Tanya Blackwood, CRO | Authority Systems Group™*

**Why this sequence works**: The three-email arc moves from reconnection (no ask) → education + soft offer → referral request + gentle close. This mirrors the emotional journey of a past client who may be dormant but still holds goodwill. We're not asking them to need us — we're reminding them we exist and that their network might need us.

**Expected response pattern**: The highest response rate typically comes from Email 1 (surprise and warmth) and Email 2 (relevance trigger). Email 3 catches the stragglers and generates the most referral action.

**Revenue targets**:
- Conservative: 3 new matters at avg $5,500 = $16,500
- Target: 6 new matters at avg $5,500 = $33,000
- Stretch: 10 new matters at avg $5,500 = $55,000
- Timeline: 30–45 days from first send

**Modification emphasis**: Email 2 specifically surfaces the post-decree modification triggers because this is Melissa's highest-probability fast cash play. A past client with a changing custody situation already trusts the firm — they just need a reason to call.

**Phase 2 consideration**: If initial send (most recent 3-4 years of clients) generates strong response, expand to full past client list. Segment and customize subject lines for clients whose cases are more than 5 years old.
