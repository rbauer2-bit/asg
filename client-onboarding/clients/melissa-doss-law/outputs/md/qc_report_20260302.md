## Gate 3 — Revenue Logic
*Lens: Tanya Blackwood, CRO*

**Status: REVISION REQUIRED → APPROVED**

Evidence of strength:
- All three Fast Cash Sprint strategies have conservative/target/stretch revenue estimates with stated assumptions. Path from strategy to action to dollar is traceable for all three.
- Fast Cash strategies are genuinely fast: reactivation (30 days), referral network (60 days), financing (14 days setup). No "long-term play" masquerading as fast cash.
- KPI targets are grounded in firm size estimates (6-person NKY firm, ~$40k/month current revenue, 12 consults/month). Conservative targets are achievable without extraordinary effort.

**Gaps identified:**
1. The $306k annual projection in Strategy 3 (Legal Financing) reads as a 90-day claim. The language "from a change that takes one week to implement" immediately follows "$306k annual revenue increase" — a reader will interpret this as a near-term projection, not an annualized one. This needs to be made explicit.
2. Q2 LinkedIn strategy: budget and activity described, no CPL target or minimum booking conversion. A brand-awareness activity without a revenue anchor violates Gate 3.
3. Q3 Paid LinkedIn: "$500–$1,000/month" budget stated, no expected return or conversion target. Need CPL target and minimum monthly consult booking floor.
4. Q2–Q4 strategic plan sections: activities enumerated, no quarterly revenue checkpoints. Client cannot evaluate quarterly performance without them.

**Remediation:**
- Clarify $306k as annualized projection with explicit assumption statement
- Add LinkedIn CPL target and monthly consultation booking floor to Q2
- Add paid LinkedIn CPL target and conversion floor to Q3
- Add quarterly revenue checkpoints to Q2, Q3, Q4

**Gate 3: APPROVED post-remediation**
— Tanya Blackwood, CRO | Authority Systems Group™
