## [SECTION 1 — ROGER]

**HEADLINE:**
Roger — Director, Authority Systems Group™

**BODY:**
Roger has spent years building and refining one thing: a proprietary system for turning professional service expertise into market authority that converts — before the sales conversation begins.

The Authority Conversion Protocol™ is the result of that work. It is not a repackaged funnel framework or a set of marketing principles assembled into a deck. It is a governing doctrine — built on decision science, behavioral economics, and cognitive psychology — that defines how high-consideration buying decisions are actually made and provides a replicable operational system for engineering those decisions in favor of the right provider.

Roger's position is simple: most professional service marketing fails because it operates downstream of the buyer's decision — trying to persuade at the point of sale what could have been completed long before. The ACP moves the entire game upstream.

Every client engagement is directed by Roger. He reviews every Authority Blueprint™ personally. He signs off on every strategic recommendation. The work bears his name because it meets his standard.
