## [SECTION 2 — WHAT HAPPENS IN THE SESSION]

**HEADLINE:**
A half-day that produces a full strategic foundation.

**BODY:**

**Pre-Session (1 week before)**
You complete a structured intake document covering: your primary service, your best clients (the ones you want more of), your current marketing situation, named competitors, and the objections or hesitations you hear most often in consultations. Roger reviews the intake before the session begins.

---

**The Session (3-4 hours)**

The Intensive covers four areas in sequence:

**1. Avatar Profile Deep Dive**
Roger and Dr. Raymond Cross map the psychological profile of your highest-value prospect — not demographics, belief barriers. The specific fear driving their search. The trigger event that made them start looking. The internal narrative they're running before they find you. The self-image they're protecting in the decision.

**2. Enemy Belief Identification**
The Enemy Belief is the misdiagnosis your prospect is currently operating on — the frame that's preventing them from seeing the value of your service clearly. Identifying it precisely is the most important single step in the entire system. It determines the hook of every piece of content you'll produce.

**3. Full Belief-to-Buy Map — Both Tracks**
Every stage of the Belief Track (B1-B5): what the prospect thinks at each stage, and the specific content move required to advance them.
Every stage of the Emotion Track (E1-E5): what the prospect feels at each stage, and the transition trigger that moves them to the next emotional state.
Primary Stall Point identified: the specific moment in the sequence where your avatar hesitates — and why.

**4. Content Architecture Brief**
Eight to twelve named, titled content pieces mapped to their specific stage pairs. For each piece: the working title, the core belief it moves, the format, and the platform priority. This is the brief your content team or contractor executes against — not a general direction, a specific brief.

---

**Post-Session Deliverable (delivered within 5 business days)**

Everything produced in the session is documented, formatted, and delivered as a structured PDF:

- Avatar Belief Profile (4-6 pages)
- Belief-to-Buy Framework™ Map — both tracks with full avatar monologues for each stage
- Master Content Map — all 10 stage pairs with content guidance
- Primary Stall Point analysis
- Content Architecture Brief — 8-12 named pieces, mapped and ready to assign
- Convergence Statement — the specific conditions that must be true simultaneously for your avatar to hire you
