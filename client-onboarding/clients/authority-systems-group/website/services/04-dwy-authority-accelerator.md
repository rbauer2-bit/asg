**You're the right fit for the Accelerator if:**
- You want to be personally involved in building your authority positioning — not just receive a finished document
- You have the capacity for one weekly session and 3-5 hours of implementation work per week
- You want to understand the Belief-to-Buy Framework™ well enough to apply it yourself — not just consume it

## [SECTION 2 — HOW IT WORKS]

**HEADLINE:**
90 days. Weekly sessions. Live deliverables at every stage.

**BODY:**

**The structure is simple: we build in sequence, nothing skipped.**

---

**Phase 1 — Weeks 1-3: Market Position & Belief Architecture**

Roger and Daniel Frost lead three weekly sessions covering:
- Your competitive landscape — named competitors, gap analysis, your positioning opportunity
- Your avatar profile — the specific psychological profile of your highest-value prospect
- Your buyer's Belief-to-Buy map — the full B1-B5 / E1-E5 architecture for your primary service
- Your Authority Statement — the positioning line that precedes every piece of content you produce

*Output: Completed Market Analysis + Belief-to-Buy Framework™ Map (Sections 3 + 4 of the Authority Blueprint™)*

---

**Phase 2 — Weeks 4-6: Strategy & Revenue Activation**

Daniel Frost leads the 12-month strategic plan. Tanya Blackwood leads the Fast Cash Sprint.

- Month-by-month Q1 execution plan built with you in real time
- Fast Cash Sprint: three revenue opportunities from your existing assets, with revenue math and 30-day activation plans
- KPI targets established: baseline, 90-day, 6-month, 12-month

*Output: 1-Year Strategic Plan + Fast Cash Sprint + KPI Dashboard (Sections 5, 6, 7)*

---

**Phase 3 — Weeks 7-9: Content Architecture & Email Sequences**

Vivienne Carr leads content strategy and email production.

- Your Content Authority Strategy™: four pillars, 10+ named titled content pieces, long-form anchor brief
- Four fully written email sequences: reactivation, referral capture, new lead welcome, referral partner outreach
- Content calendar for Q1 built in full
- Brand voice guide finalized

*Output: Content Authority Strategy™ + Email Sequences (Sections 8 + 10)*

---

**Phase 4 — Weeks 10-12: Systems, Assets & Launch**

- Lead magnet title, full table of contents, and distribution plan
- Referral program mechanics and compliance structure
- CRM automation logic mapped (execution optional — see Marcus Chen)
- Priority actions for Days 1-30 after the Accelerator ends
- Final QC review: all four gates

*Output: Lead Magnet & Referral Program + Priority Services & Next Steps (Sections 11 + 12)*
