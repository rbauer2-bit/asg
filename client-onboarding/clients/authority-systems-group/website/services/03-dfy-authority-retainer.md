## [SECTION 4 — HOW ENGAGEMENT WORKS]

**HEADLINE:**
The operational structure.

**BODY:**

**Month 1 — System Configuration**
If starting with a completed Authority Blueprint™: Month 1 is system setup — CRM configuration, content voice guide finalization, editorial calendar structure, and baseline content production.

If starting Retainer without a prior Blueprint: The Blueprint is completed in Weeks 1-4, and Retainer execution begins in Month 2.

**Monthly Rhythm**
- Week 1: Quarterly strategy review (Month 1 of each quarter) OR content review and approval (Months 2-3)
- Weeks 1-4: Content production, email campaign builds, CRM updates
- End of month: Delivery package — all content, campaigns, and updates for review and approval
- Client approves, requests revisions, or both within 5 business days
- Approved content is scheduled for deployment

**Communication**
- Monthly written report: content produced, campaigns running, KPI actuals vs. targets
- Quarterly strategy session with Roger: 60-90 minutes, video call
- Async communication for approvals, questions, and urgent items
