## [SECTION 2 — WHAT'S IN IT]

**HEADLINE:**
Five modules. The complete doctrine — applied.

**BODY:**

---

**Module 1 — The Diagnosis: Why Your Marketing Isn't Working**

The foundational reframe. This module teaches the enemy belief most professional service businesses hold about their marketing — and replaces it with the correct diagnosis.

Covered: The five belief conditions required before any high-consideration purchase. The two parallel decision tracks (cognitive and emotional) that must both resolve before action occurs. Why buying is a completion, not a moment. The real cost of producing content that mismatches the buyer's belief stage.

*Workbook exercise: Identify the current enemy belief your prospects are operating on.*

---

**Module 2 — The Avatar: Mapping the Buyer's Psychological Journey**

How to build a belief-accurate avatar profile — not a demographic description, a psychological one. This module teaches how to identify trigger events, belief barriers, internal narratives, and the self-image your prospect is protecting throughout the decision.

Covered: The difference between demographics and belief position. How to identify the trigger event that initiates search. The seven questions that reveal the emotional state at each stage. How to write the internal monologue your avatar is running before they find you.

*Workbook exercise: Complete a full avatar belief profile for your primary service.*

---

**Module 3 — The Map: The Belief-to-Buy Framework™**

The complete dual-track system. This is the core of the ACP — the five-stage Belief Track and the five-stage Emotion Track, how they run in parallel, what moves a prospect from one stage to the next, and what happens when the sequence is skipped.

Covered: All ten stages (B1-B5, E1-E5) with definitions, avatar monologues, and transition triggers for each. The Primary Stall Point — where most professional service buyers hesitate and why. The Convergence Statement — what must be true simultaneously on both tracks for the buy to occur.

*Workbook exercise: Complete the full B/E map for your primary service.*

---

**Module 4 — The Architecture: Building Content That Moves Beliefs**

How to turn the belief map into a content production system. This module teaches content planning, brief writing, and stage-pair assignment so that every piece produced has a specific job in the buyer's journey.

Covered: The Master Content Map. How to assign stage pairs to existing and planned content. The Content Authority Strategy™ structure: four pillars, named titled pieces, long-form anchor briefs. The mandatory content quality test (the five-check system applied to every piece before it's finalized).

*Workbook exercise: Build a 12-piece content brief set for your Q1 focus service.*

---

**Module 5 — The 12 Laws: The Doctrine Beneath the System**

The structural mechanics of human decision-making — the 12 Laws of Decision Architecture formalized by Dr. Raymond Cross — taught in sequence, with specific application guidance for each Law.

Covered: All 12 Laws with definitions, mechanisms, and application notes. How to trace any content piece to a specific Law. Common Law violations in professional service marketing (and what they cost). The Law of Behavioral Signaling: how to use campaign analytics as belief-position data.

*Workbook exercise: Audit three pieces of your current marketing against the 12 Laws.*
