## [SECTION 2 — WHAT'S IN IT]

**HEADLINE:**
Thirteen sections. Every one complete.

**BODY:**
The Authority Blueprint™ is built to a documented standard — the same floor applied to every engagement. No section is a placeholder. No section is a rewording of another. Every page earns its place.

---

**Section 1 — Executive Summary**
Roger's read of your three core opportunities, the strategic frame, and the 90-day vision. Written for someone who needs the most important things said once, precisely.

**Section 2 — Director's Briefing**
Roger's direct read of your specific situation — where you are, why the sequence matters, and what he needs from you for the system to work. More candid than the executive summary. This is Roger speaking directly to you.

**Section 3 — Market Analysis & Competitive Positioning**
Your named competitors, analyzed specifically — not in general terms. Your market size, your avatar profile (the psychological profile of the buyer most likely to hire you and refer others), the positioning gap your competitors have left open, and your Authority Statement — the positioning line that makes the case for you before you have to make it yourself.

**Section 4 — Belief-to-Buy Framework™ Map**
The full buyer belief architecture for your primary service. Every stage of the Belief Track (B1-B5) with your avatar's internal monologue at each stage — the exact language in their head at each point in the decision. Every stage of the Emotion Track (E1-E5) with the emotional state and the transition trigger. The Master Content Map. The Primary Stall Point — the specific moment where most of your prospects hesitate and why.

**Section 5 — 1-Year Strategic Plan**
Month-by-month execution across all four quarters. Not quarterly themes — actual named actions, specific deliverables, and accountable owners for every month of the year. Q1 KPI targets. Q2 KPI targets. Year-end success criteria.

**Section 6 — Fast Cash Sprint™**
Three revenue-accelerating strategies executable in 30 to 90 days — built from your existing assets (past clients, referral relationships, dormant contacts). Revenue math for each strategy: list size × realistic conversion rate × average client value. A 90-day activation calendar with checkbox implementation format. This is where most clients start.

**Section 7 — KPI Dashboard**
Five measurement categories. Baseline numbers. 90-day targets. 6-month targets. 12-month targets. A dashboard architecture and monthly reporting structure that tells you whether the system is working before you run out of patience.

**Section 8 — Content Authority Strategy™**
Your content authority position. Four content pillars with volume allocation. Ten or more named, titled content pieces for your Q1 focus service — mapped to the specific belief stage pairs they serve. A long-form anchor piece brief. Format priority matrix by platform.

**Section 9 — Niche Website Strategy** *(when a domain asset exists or is proposed)*
Platform specifications, site architecture, local SEO keyword targets, and performance milestones. Built by Iris Nolan, ASG's CTO. Included when a niche digital asset is part of the strategy.

**Section 10 — Email Sequences**
Four fully written email sequences — minimum. Every email complete: subject line, full body copy, sign-off, compliance footer. Deployment-ready. No structural placeholders. No "[insert email here]."

- Sequence 1: Past Client Reactivation (5 emails over 18 days)
- Sequence 2: Referral Capture from happy clients (3 emails over 12 days)
- Sequence 3: Welcome Sequence for new leads (5 emails over 7 days)
- Sequence 4: Professional Referral Partner Outreach (3 emails over 21 days)

Additional sequences added when fast cash priorities require them.

**Section 11 — Lead Magnet & Referral Program**
A fully titled lead magnet with complete table of contents (10-12 sections), format specification, distribution plan, and design notes. A referral program with mechanics, compliance structure, thank-you and incentive design, capture process, and monthly tracking framework.

**Section 12 — Priority Services & Next Steps**
Owner-assigned action items for Days 1-30, 31-60, and 61-90. Three columns: what ASG provides, what the client provides, and the deadline. Meeting cadence. No ambiguity about who does what or when.

**Section 13 — QC Gate Sign-Offs**
Every Authority Blueprint™ passes through a four-gate quality assurance process before it reaches you. Strategic Alignment (Daniel Frost). Messaging Integrity (Vivienne Carr + Dr. Raymond Cross). Revenue Logic (Tanya Blackwood). Delivery Standard (Preston Adler). Section 13 is the written record of all four approvals.

## [SECTION 3 — WHAT THIS IS NOT]

**HEADLINE:**
What the Authority Blueprint™ is not.

**BODY:**
It is not a 200-page document padded with market research that applies to every business in your category. Volume does not equal value. Redundancy is not thoroughness.

The Blueprint floor is set by a documented standard and has never been met with a document that repeated itself. Every section adds something the previous sections haven't already said. Every page earns its place.

It is not a generic strategy template with your name in the header. Every named competitor is named. Every avatar monologue is written for your specific buyer. Every revenue estimate shows its math.

It is not a pitch deck for a retainer. The Authority Blueprint™ is a complete, standalone deliverable. You own it. You can implement it without ASG. If you choose to engage ASG for ongoing implementation, the Blueprint is the strategy we execute against — but the document is yours regardless.
