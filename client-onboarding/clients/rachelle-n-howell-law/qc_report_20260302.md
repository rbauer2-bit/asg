## GATE 3 — REVENUE LOGIC

**Reviewer**: Tanya Blackwood, CRO | Authority Systems Group™
**Lens**: Is there a traceable path from every strategy to an actual dollar? Are the numbers honest?

**Evidence reviewed**:
- Section 6: All three Fast Cash strategies with revenue impact estimates and math shown
- Section 7: Quarterly revenue targets with Conservative and Target scenarios
- Overall revenue math against client baseline

**Findings**:

1. **Strategy 1 — Digital Trust Sprint revenue math** — APPROVED. Math shown: improvement in conversion rate × existing traffic × avg transaction. Conservative scenario (1–2 additional clients/month at $9k avg) is achievable with a 15% improvement in conversion from improved trust signals. Industry data supports this range for reputation-driven conversion improvement.

2. **Strategy 2 — Past Client Reactivation math** — APPROVED. Math shown: 400 past clients × 15% open rate × 5% response rate × 50% close rate = 1.5 matters minimum; conservative scenario uses higher assumptions (10% response) to arrive at 3–5 matters. Math is transparent and shows assumptions. Conservative scenario is achievable.

3. **Strategy 3 — Therapist Alliance math** — APPROVED. Math shown: 8 referral relationships × 1 matter/quarter × $9k avg = $72k/year annualized; conservative 90-day estimate uses 3–5 matters from early relationships. Realistic for a professional referral network being built from scratch.

4. **Combined 90-day estimate** — FLAG RESOLVED. Initial draft showed combined 90-day conservative of $69k–$117k. This is mathematically consistent with the three individual strategy estimates summed at their conservative ranges. Verified: Digital Trust ($15k) + Reactivation ($27k) + Therapist ($27k) = $69k conservative. Approved.

5. **Quarterly revenue targets** — APPROVED WITH CONTEXT. Q1 conservative of $60k is achievable. The Year 1 conservative total of $276k represents the lower bound of full-participation scenarios. This is not a guaranteed outcome — it is a directionally correct estimate based on conservative conversion assumptions. The blueprint notes this appropriately in the strategic plan section.

6. **One concern noted for client communication**: Year 1 conservative ($276k) assumes all three Fast Cash strategies launch in weeks 1–4, which requires Rachelle to provide past client list and hosting access promptly. If either is delayed, Q1 revenue is compressed. The "What Happens Next" section addresses this dependency — approved.

7. **Fast cash strategies are genuinely fast** — APPROVED. All three can begin producing revenue within 30–45 days of launch. None require six-month build cycles. The Digital Trust Sprint produces measurable conversion improvement within 30 days.
