## NOTE ON 3-YEAR AND 5-YEAR PLANS

The `STRATEGIC_PLAN_3YR.md` and `STRATEGIC_PLAN_5YR.md` are **not included in the Authority Blueprint™**. They are separate deliverables produced after the Blueprint engagement is running and results are being generated. Including them in the initial Blueprint dilutes the focus on Year 1 execution. If a client specifically requests long-range planning as part of the engagement, these are added as an appendix — clearly labeled as a secondary deliverable, not the primary plan.

---

## NOTE ON BOARD CHAIR LETTER

The Margaret Harlow Board Chair letter (`EXECUTIVE_SUMMARY.md` Section 1) is **optional**. For most engagements, it adds formality without adding strategic value. Include it when:
- The engagement is a premium tier (e.g., $5k+ monthly retainer)
- The client has expressed they value a formal, board-level endorsement
- The document will be used internally to present to the client's partners or investors

When included, it appears before Section 1 as an unnumbered letter page.

## SECTION-BY-SECTION FORMATTING SPECIFICATIONS

### Cover Page
See COVER PAGE SPECIFICATION below.

### Interior Section Headers

Every major section opens with:
- Full-width ASG Blue (#25aae1) horizontal rule, 2pt
- Section number + title (ASG Blue, 22-24pt, Calibri Bold)
- Prepared-by attribution (charcoal italic, 11pt)
- Section introductory paragraph that states what this section does and why it matters

### Body Text
- Font: Georgia or Cambria, 11pt
- Color: #333333
- Line spacing: 1.4
- Paragraph spacing: 6pt after
- Margins: 1.25" left/right, 1" top/bottom

### Callout / Blockquote Boxes
- Background: #F5F5F5
- Left border: 4pt ASG Blue (#25aae1)
- Font: Calibri italic, 11pt
- Color: ASG Blue for persona quotes
- Maximum 2 per page

### Persona Sign-Off Format
```
[Sign-off quote in italics]
— [Full Name], [Title] | Authority Systems Group™
```
Present at the close of every major section.

### Tables
- Header row: #25aae1 background, white text, Calibri Bold 11pt
- Alternating rows: white / #F5F5F5
- All tables include a title line above in Calibri Bold 12pt Charcoal

### Email Sequences Section Formatting
Each email within Section 10 opens with:
```
EMAIL [#] — [EMAIL NAME IN CAPS]
Day [X] | Stage: [B/E stage pair]
```
Then: Subject line in bold → full body copy → compliance footer

### Page Headers and Footers
- Header: ASG logo (left) | "Authority Blueprint™ — [Client Name]" (right, 9pt charcoal)
- Footer: "Authority Systems Group™ — Confidential. Prepared exclusively for [Client Name]." (center) | Page number (right)
