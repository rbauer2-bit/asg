## FRAMEWORKS REFERENCED

- **Dopamine Ladder Framework** — six sequential neurological reward states that must be triggered in order:
  - **Level 1 — Stimulation**: Stop the scroll via visual, verbal, or conceptual novelty (0–2 seconds)
  - **Level 2 — Captivation**: Trigger an irresistible internal question (curiosity loop)
  - **Level 3 — Anticipation**: Keep the brain guessing by delaying resolution (breadcrumbing, misdirection)
  - **Level 4 — Validation**: Deliver a surprising-but-true insight that rewards continued attention
  - **Level 5 — Affection**: Create emotional trust and likeability through authenticity and warmth
  - **Level 6 — Revelation**: Close with an identity-level hook that conditions the audience to binge future content
- **Avatar Psychology Map** — desires, fears, identity, and motivational triggers drive every topic selection and emotional angle
- **Algorithm Trigger Engineering** — platform-specific signals (watch time, completion rate, shares, comments) built into pacing and structure
- **Content Variety Rhythm** — story, education, contrarian insight, problem-solution, and identity content rotated to prevent fatigue
- **Variant Framework** — Value Bomb version (rapid-fire) and Contrarian version (challenging industry beliefs) produced for every day

#### TASK 2: 30 Complete Scripts — Dopamine Ladder Framework

For each of the 30 days, produce a complete script following all six levels:

**LEVEL 1 — STIMULATION (0–2 seconds)**
Goal: Stop the scroll via visual, verbal, or conceptual novelty.
Output:
- 1 main hook
- 2 alternate hook variations

Use pattern breaks, paradox, contrast, motion, or shock-value curiosity.

**LEVEL 2 — CAPTIVATION (Curiosity Loop)**
Goal: Trigger an irresistible internal question.
Output: 1–2 sentences that provoke curiosity relevant to this specific avatar.

**LEVEL 3 — ANTICIPATION (Suspense & Delay)**
Goal: Keep their brain guessing by delaying resolution.
Output: 3–5 sentences that breadcrumb, tease, misdirect, or add uncertainty.

**LEVEL 4 — VALIDATION (Aha Moment)**
Goal: Deliver a surprising-but-true insight that rewards the viewer.
Output: 2–4 sentences showing clarity, insight, or solution frameworks.

**LEVEL 5 — AFFECTION (Human Bonding)**
Goal: Make them emotionally like and trust the creator.
Output: 2–3 sentences revealing warmth, authenticity, relatability, or helpfulness.

**LEVEL 6 — REVELATION (Identity + Brand Hook)**
Goal: Condition their brain to crave more of this content.
Output: A branded closing line that creates inevitability and encourages binge-watching.
