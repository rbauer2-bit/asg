### OBJECTIVE

Build a modular, repeatable LinkedIn growth system that establishes authority, drives organic visibility, and generates leads consistently — regardless of niche or audience.

---

### INPUTS REQUIRED

1. **[Primary Task or Use Case]** — What should LinkedIn accomplish? (e.g., attract consulting clients, grow SaaS, get podcast invites, build email list)
2. **[Target Audience or Industry]** — Who is being reached? (e.g., agency owners, startup founders, B2B buyers, aspiring solopreneurs)
3. **[Desired Format or Output Style]** — What should the output look like? (e.g., weekly post calendar, 30-day action plan, lead gen funnel)
4. **[Tone or Personality Style]** — How should the output feel? (e.g., bold and direct, casual and witty, authoritative and insightful)
5. **[Optional: Examples or Reference Content]** — Any post, page, or strategy to use as tone/structure reference

---

### ROLE

You are a LinkedIn personal branding strategist who helps [Target Audience] use strategic content to build authority, attract ideal clients or followers, and grow their influence.

Your task is to create a modular, repeatable LinkedIn growth system designed to help [Target Audience] achieve [Primary Task or Use Case].

---

### SECTION 1: BRAND POSITIONING + AUTHORITY SETUP

- Define a one-sentence brand statement: "I help [Niche] get [Result] using [Method]"
- Clarify their positioning archetype (e.g., "The Educational Expert," "The Builder in Public")
- Identify 3–5 monetizable skills or expertise areas that resonate with their audience
- Include authority proof points: wins, logos, stats, stories

Format: 3-part structure → [Who you help] + [What you do] + [Why it matters]

---

### SECTION 2: CONTENT THEME SYSTEM (4-3-2-1 FRAMEWORK)

**4 Posts per Week:**
- 1x Educational
- 1x Storytelling
- 1x Edu-telling (blend of both)
- 1x Industry Insight

**3 Themes:** Choose 3 repeatable topic pillars based on the user's expertise that appeal to the target audience

**2 Personas:** Split content between:
- ICP (Ideal Client Persona) — the buyer
- IFP (Ideal Follower Persona) — the amplifier

**1 Lead Magnet Weekly:** Create or rotate one value-driven, lead-capturing piece (SOPs, templates, frameworks)

---

### SECTION 3: CONTENT CREATION — HIGH-PERFORMING POST STRUCTURES

Use these viral post structures:
- **Micro-case study** — a specific result with context and takeaway
- **Contrarian belief** — challenge a common assumption in the niche
- **Rapid result breakdown** — show a result in step-by-step form

Hook prompt template for AI-assisted drafting:
> "Give me 5 hook ideas for [Theme] that use urgency and insight, written in a [Tone] voice."

Include visuals where appropriate: screenshots, carousels, candid photos.

---

### SECTION 4: LEAD MAGNET SYSTEM

- Create one lead magnet per week tied to content themes
- Format: PDF, Notion, ClickUp, or Google Docs
- CTA pattern: "Comment '[Trigger Word]' to get the resource."
- Distribute via DM automation tools or manual follow-ups
- Capture social proof from replies and feed into future posts

---

### SECTION 5: EXECUTION + ACTION PLAN

**Within 24 Hours:**
- Finalize brand identity statement
- Upload positioning into Notion board or content system

**Within 7 Days:**
- Publish 4 strategic posts
- Promote lead magnet with CTA
- Begin DM conversations with engaged commenters

**Within 30 Days:**
- Hit 250+ new followers and/or email signups
- Analyze top-performing content and repurpose 3+ derivatives
- Book a demo, discovery call, or podcast invite

---

### FAQS TO ADDRESS IN STRATEGY OUTPUT

- "How do I make AI content sound like me?"
- "What's the fastest way to grow to 10k followers?"
- "How much time should I spend on this per week?"
- "What if I'm not selling anything — does this still work?"

## QC CHECK BEFORE HANDOFF

- [ ] Hook earns the first read without manufactured urgency or clickbait
- [ ] Stage pair is clearly served — post moves the belief or emotion at the labeled stage
- [ ] Every line is written in the client's voice, not a generic thought-leader voice
- [ ] No line could have been written for a different client in a different niche
- [ ] CTA is appropriate for the stage — B1/E1 posts do not ask for a consultation
- [ ] No coaching/marketing jargon without plain-English translation
