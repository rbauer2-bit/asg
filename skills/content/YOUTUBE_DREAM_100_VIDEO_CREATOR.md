## PURPOSE

Produces YouTube video strategy and individual video production briefs using the Dream 100 model — identifying the specific channels and subscriber communities the client's ideal clients already belong to, and creating video content designed to appear in those subscribers' search results and "Up Next" queues.

Output includes both the channel-level strategy (Dream 100 channel analysis, content pillar architecture) and individual video production briefs (title, description, thumbnail brief, script outline, keyword strategy).

## FRAMEWORKS REFERENCED

- Dream 100 applied to YouTube: identify the 20 channels whose subscribers are the ideal client; create content engineered to appear in their recommended feeds
- YouTube SEO: title, description, tags, chapter markers, thumbnail psychology
- The three YouTube content types for professional service firms:
  - **Authority pillars**: Long-form (15–30 min) — the "why this matters" and "how this works" videos
  - **Search capture**: Medium-form (8–15 min) — specific question-based content with high search intent
  - **Community growth**: Shorter (5–10 min) — opinion, commentary, and perspective content that earns subscribers
- Thumbnail brief format (for a designer): focal element, text overlay, color psychology, face expression guidance

### HOW TO INVOKE THIS SKILL

This skill produces a complete YouTube Strategy Dream 100 deliverable for a prospect or client — used as high-value outreach or as a paid deliverable. It is research-first, fully written (not an outline), and deeply customized to the prospect's specific business, offer, audience, and brand voice.

**Before executing, gather:**
- Prospect's primary website or sales page URL
- Prospect's YouTube channel URL (if available)
- Prospect's LinkedIn profile URL (if available)
- Any other relevant links (podcast, lead magnet pages, sales funnels)

**Attach:** The Daniel Fazio example YouTube strategy document as the structural reference.

---

### RESEARCH PHASE (execute before writing a single word)

Visit every provided URL and extract:

**From the website or sales page:**
- Core offer (exactly what they sell)
- Target audience (who it's for)
- Problems solved
- Unique selling propositions and differentiators
- Pricing (if visible)
- Brand voice and messaging style

**From their YouTube channel (if available):**
- Topics covered
- Video style and tone
- Language and phrases commonly used
- Existing content strategy — what's working, what's missing

**From their LinkedIn (if available):**
- Personal brand positioning
- Content types and posting style
- Writing voice and energy level

Do not begin writing until research is complete. Every component must reflect what was found — not assumed.

### THE DELIVERABLE

Produce a single, complete, well-organized Markdown document with all six components fully written. This is NOT an outline. Every word the speaker would say, every email body, every lead magnet page — written out in full.

---

#### COMPONENT 1: STRATEGY OVERVIEW

Write a clear explanation of:
- How the YouTube funnel strategy works for this specific prospect
- How the different video types work together to attract, educate, and convert
- The funnel flow from discovery to conversion, mapped to the prospect's offer

---

#### COMPONENT 2: THE MEGA VSL / FREE COURSE (1-HOUR VIDEO SCRIPT)

A complete, word-for-word script for a 1-hour video that functions simultaneously as a free course and a video sales letter.

**Structure — 6 parts with timestamps:**

| Timestamp | Section |
|---|---|
| 0:00–10:00 | Part 1 |
| 10:00–25:00 | Part 2 |
| 25:00–40:00 | Part 3 |
| 40:00–50:00 | Part 4 |
| 50:00–55:00 | Part 5 |
| 55:00–60:00 | Part 6 |

The script must:
- Educate the viewer on the problem, the solution, and the prospect's specific offer
- End with a primary CTA to book a call or take the prospect's next-step action
- Include visual cues, tone directions, and screen recording descriptions where relevant

---

#### COMPONENT 3: THREE BROAD APPEAL VIDEOS (TOP OF FUNNEL)

Three videos, each 4–5 minutes, targeting broad pain points and curiosities to attract a wide audience.

**For each video, produce:**
- Full word-for-word script (hook, body, CTA)
- Title and thumbnail suggestion
- Primary CTA: watch the 1-hour Mega VSL

---

#### COMPONENT 4: THREE LEAD MAGNET VIDEOS

Three videos, each 4–5 minutes, each pitching one specific high-value lead magnet in exchange for an email address.

**For each video, produce:**
- Full word-for-word script (hook, body, CTA)
- Title and thumbnail suggestion
- Primary CTA: download the lead magnet

---

#### COMPONENT 5: THREE COMPLETE LEAD MAGNETS (THE ACTUAL PDF CONTENT)

For each of the 3 lead magnet videos, write the complete content of the lead magnet itself.

**Each lead magnet must be:**
- 6–8 pages of valuable, actionable content
- Structured as a PDF guide (written content only — no design required)
- Highly specific and immediately useful to the prospect's target audience
- Deeply customized to the prospect's offer and market position

---

#### COMPONENT 6: THREE 10-EMAIL OPT-IN SEQUENCES

For each of the 3 lead magnets, write a complete 10-email nurture sequence.

**Each email must include:**
- Subject line
- Full body copy

**Sequence logic:**
- Emails sent daily over 10 days
- Goal: provide value, build trust, drive the subscriber to watch the Mega VSL or book a call
- Each sequence must be distinct — different angle, different lead-in, same destination

---

### VOICE AND TONE STANDARDS

The copy must sound like the prospect wrote it themselves:
- Match their level of formality, energy, and directness
- Use language and terminology natural to their industry
- If they're casual and conversational, be casual and conversational
- If they're formal and professional, be formal and professional

**Do not use these phrases — ever:**
- "The kicker?"
- "It is not about X. It is about Y."
- "And the best part?"
- "Bottom line"
- "Here is the thing..."
- "At the end of the day..."
- Any other AI writing pattern that flattens voice
