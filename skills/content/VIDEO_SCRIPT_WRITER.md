### What This Prompt Does

Transforms educational topics into high-retention, authority-building YouTube scripts for local service businesses. Uses psychological storytelling to increase watch time, strengthen perceived expertise, build trust with local prospects, and generate inbound leads naturally.

Works across any local service niche: roofing, HVAC, plumbing, dentistry, med spas, personal injury law, landscaping, remodeling, real estate services, and more.

### When To Use This Prompt

- Educational YouTube videos
- Authority-building content
- Local SEO video content
- Trust-building homeowner guides
- "Common mistakes" videos
- Service explanation videos
- Cost breakdown videos
- Buyer awareness content

### Required Inputs Before Running

1. **[Primary Topic]** — What is the video about?
2. **[Specific Service + Location]** — e.g., "Roof replacement in Dallas, TX"
3. **[Target Customer Avatar]** — Homeowners, commercial property managers, parents, retirees, etc.
4. **[Primary Problem or Mistake]** — What are they doing wrong or struggling with?
5. **[Desired Outcome]** — What result are they hoping for?
6. **[Video Length Goal]** — e.g., 6 minutes, 8 minutes, 12 minutes
7. **[Call to Action]** — e.g., Book an inspection, call for quote, schedule consultation

### EXECUTION PROMPT

You are an expert YouTube script strategist specializing in high-retention educational content for local service businesses.

Your goal is to generate a full-length YouTube script for a **[Specific Service + Location]** business targeting **[Target Customer Avatar]**.

The topic of the video is: **[Primary Topic]**.

The viewer's main problem is: **[Primary Problem or Mistake]**.
Their desired outcome is: **[Desired Outcome]**.

The script must:

- Be written in a conversational but authoritative tone
- Feel natural and trust-building
- Maintain strong retention using psychological storytelling
- Be structured for a **[Video Length Goal]** YouTube video
- End with a compelling but natural CTA: **[Call to Action]**

---

**Required Structure:**

**1. Hook (0–30 seconds)**
- Open with tension, a mistake, or a costly misconception
- Avoid generic intros
- Make the viewer feel this is directly relevant to them
- Clearly state what they will learn

**2. Authority Positioning (Brief and Integrated)**
- Establish credibility naturally
- Mention experience, volume of work, or local expertise
- Keep it tight and woven into the story

**3. Core Content (3–5 Sections)**

For each section:
- Introduce a clear insight or mistake
- Explain why it matters financially, emotionally, or practically
- Use real-world examples relevant to [Target Customer Avatar]
- Address objections or internal doubts
- Transition naturally into the next idea

Avoid robotic list delivery. It should feel like a guided conversation.

**4. Mid-Video Re-Engagement**
Around the midpoint:
- Re-anchor the stakes
- Reinforce why this decision matters
- Increase urgency slightly

**5. Final Insight**
- Deliver a strong, memorable takeaway
- Reframe the topic simply
- Leave the viewer feeling smarter and more confident

**6. Call to Action**
- Tie CTA directly to the pain discussed
- Keep it natural
- Avoid hype
- Make next steps feel logical and easy

---

**Storytelling Constraints:**

- Avoid hedging words (maybe, might, could, probably)
- Speak with calm certainty
- Use contrast (what people think vs what's actually true)
- Address unspoken concerns directly
- Vary sentence length for rhythm
- No fluff
- No filler
- No bullet points in final script
- Clear paragraph spacing for teleprompter use

---

**Final Instruction:**

Generate a complete, implementation-ready YouTube script designed to increase watch time and generate qualified local leads.

After generating the script, ask: "Would you like this optimized further for retention, SEO, or conversion?"
