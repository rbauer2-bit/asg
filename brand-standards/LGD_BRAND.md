**Organization**: Authority Systems Group™
**Parent Company**: Legal Growth Dynamics (internal only — never in client materials)

**Trademark symbol**: Use ™ on first mention of "Authority Systems Group" and "Authority Blueprint" in every document

## COLOR PALETTE

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| ASG Blue | #25aae1 | Primary headers, accent bars, cover page elements, highlights |
| ASG Charcoal | #58585a | Body text, secondary headers, footer text |
| White | #FFFFFF | Page background, text on dark backgrounds |
| Light Gray | #F5F5F5 | Section backgrounds, callout boxes |
| Dark Overlay | #1a1a1a | Cover page background, full-bleed sections |

**Color Rules**:
- Never use more than 2 accent colors on a single page
- Blue is for emphasis and hierarchy — not decoration
- Charcoal is the default for all body copy
- Cover pages: Dark background (#1a1a1a) with white text and blue accents

---

## TYPOGRAPHY

**Primary Heading (H1)**: Montserrat Bold or Calibri Bold — 24-28pt — ASG Blue (#25aae1)
**Secondary Heading (H2)**: Montserrat SemiBold or Calibri Bold — 18-20pt — ASG Charcoal (#58585a)
**Section Heading (H3)**: Montserrat Medium or Calibri — 14-16pt — ASG Charcoal
**Body Text**: Georgia or Cambria — 11-12pt — #333333
**Callout/Pull Quote**: Montserrat Italic or Calibri Italic — 13pt — ASG Blue
**Footer Text**: Calibri — 9pt — #888888

**If system fonts only**: Use Calibri for headings, Georgia for body. Never use Comic Sans, Papyrus, or Arial.

## DOCUMENT STRUCTURE STANDARDS

### Cover Page Must Include:
1. ASG logo (centered, white version on dark background)
2. Document title (large, white, Montserrat Bold)
3. Subtitle (ASG Blue accent color)
4. Client name ("Prepared exclusively for [Client Name]")
5. Date (Month Year format)
6. "Confidential" notation
7. Director attribution ("Under the direction of Roger, Director")

### Interior Page Elements:
- Header: ASG logo left, document title right (small)
- Footer: "Authority Systems Group™ — Confidential. Prepared exclusively for [Client Name]." | Page number
- Section divider: Full-width ASG Blue line (#25aae1, 2pt) before major sections
- Callout boxes: Light gray background (#F5F5F5), left border in ASG Blue (4pt)

## VOICE & TONE STANDARDS

### Always:
- Specific over general
- Outcomes over activities
- Confident without arrogance
- Direct without being cold
- Sophisticated without being academic

### Never:
- Generic claims ("quality service", "comprehensive solutions")
- Passive voice in recommendations
- Hedging language that undermines confidence ("might", "could possibly consider")
- Marketing buzzwords that don't survive plain-English translation
- First-person plural that sounds like a corporation ("we believe that...")
  - Use instead: team member attributed voice ("As Daniel notes in the strategy review...")

---

## PERSONA ATTRIBUTION STANDARDS

When attributing content to a team member:

**In-document**: 
> *"Daniel Frost, our Chief Strategy Officer, identified a critical positioning gap that [Competitor] has left uncontested..."*

**Section sign-offs**:
> *— Daniel Frost, CSO | Authority Systems Group™*

**Photo placeholders** (for printed spiral-bound version):
- 2x2 inch headshot placeholder in rounded circle format
- Charcoal name text below
- Blue title text
- Short 2-line bio

---

## WHAT NEVER APPEARS IN CLIENT MATERIALS

- The word "AI" or any AI tool name
- "Automated" in reference to content production
- "Template" in reference to any deliverable
- Legal Growth Dynamics name or LGD branding
- Any reference to a system or tool producing the work
- Price of the engagement (let the value speak)
- Comparative claims about other agencies ("better than Big 4" — show it, don't say it)
