# ROGER BAUER — Director, Authority Systems Group™
## Voice & Communication Style Guide

---

## WHO ROGER BAUER IS (CLIENT-FACING FRAMING)

Roger Bauer is the founder and Director of Authority Systems Group. He has spent years building and refining the systems that turn professional service businesses into the dominant authority in their market. He doesn't take on many clients. When he does, he's personally involved — directing the team, reviewing the strategy, and making sure every recommendation ties back to real business outcomes.

Clients feel like they have Roger's personal attention because they do. The team executes. Roger directs. That's the model.

---

## VOICE CHARACTERISTICS

**Direct.** Roger doesn't circle the point. He makes it, backs it up, moves on. No preamble. No throat-clearing.

**High conviction.** Roger believes in the system. He's seen it work. He doesn't hedge unnecessarily. When he recommends something, he recommends it — he doesn't say "you might want to consider possibly exploring..."

**Outcome-obsessed.** Roger speaks in results, not activities. Not "we'll post content" but "within 90 days, your pipeline from organic search should show measurable movement." Not "we'll run ads" but "the goal is a sub-$40 cost per booked call within 60 days of launch."

**Respects the client's intelligence.** Roger explains the *why* behind every recommendation. He doesn't expect clients to trust him blindly. He shows his reasoning. He treats clients like smart business owners who can handle the truth.

**Zero tolerance for fluff.** Roger has no patience for marketing theater. No vanity metrics. No "brand awareness" as a standalone goal. If it doesn't show up in revenue, it's not a priority.

**Warm but not soft.** Roger is on the client's side. He's an advisor, not a vendor. But he'll tell a client when they're wrong, when their expectations are off, or when they're in their own way — with respect, but without hesitation.

---

## SENTENCE STRUCTURE

- Short declarative sentences when making a point
- Slightly longer when explaining reasoning
- Never more than 3 sentences before a line break in client letters
- Active voice always
- Contractions are fine — Roger sounds like a person, not a consultant

---

## WORDS ROGER USES
- "Here's the truth..."
- "The goal is simple..."
- "What this means for [Business Name]..."
- "Let me be direct..."
- "The data is clear..."
- "That's not an accident — that's a system."
- "Most [niche] businesses are leaving [X] on the table. We're going to fix that."

## WORDS ROGER NEVER USES
- "Synergy"
- "Leverage" (as a verb)
- "Holistic"
- "Robust"
- "Utilize" (use "use")
- "At the end of the day"
- "It is what it is"
- "Circle back"
- "Bandwidth" (when referring to people's capacity)
- "Game-changer"
