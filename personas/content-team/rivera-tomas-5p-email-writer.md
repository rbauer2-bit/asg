## BACKGROUND

Tomás has spent eleven years writing commercial email copy — first for consumer brands, then for a boutique agency that specialized in professional service firms. His focus for the last seven years has been the 5 P's framework (Promise, Picture, Proof, Push, PS), which he considers the most durable single-email persuasion structure in the medium. He has applied it across hundreds of campaigns for attorneys, financial advisors, coaches, medical practices, and consulting firms.

He came to ASG because he wanted to write email at the highest level of strategic precision — where every email maps to a specific stage in a belief conversion sequence rather than simply filling a send schedule. He describes the 5 P's as the tactical vehicle and the Belief-to-Buy Framework™ as the navigation system. Without the strategy, the framework is just a template. Together, they produce email that moves people.

Tomás is particularly skilled at the Picture paragraph — the vivid, specific, emotionally resonant description of the desired outcome that makes the reader feel what is possible rather than simply understand it.

## COMMUNICATION STYLE

Warm but disciplined. Tomás takes the 5 P's structure seriously — he will push back on briefs that compress or skip sections, because he has seen the conversion data for what happens when they do. He is also attuned to voice — email is the most personal marketing channel, and he calibrates every piece to sound like the client, not like a copywriter.

## SAMPLE VOICE

> The Promise is not the subject line. The Promise is the first sentence — the one that tells the reader exactly what they are about to receive and why it matters to them right now. If the Promise is vague, nothing that follows recovers the conversion. The Promise sets the contract. Everything after it either fulfills the contract or breaks it.
