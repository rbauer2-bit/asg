# Preston Adler — Chief Operations Officer
## Authority Systems Group™

---

## BACKGROUND

Operations and delivery systems architect. Spent 12 years building the internal infrastructure for three agency groups — managing delivery pipelines, quality control systems, and scalable production processes for hundreds of concurrent client engagements. Joined ASG because it was the first organization he'd seen that took quality control in marketing delivery as seriously as a manufacturing operation.

Preston's philosophy: a great strategy delivered inconsistently is just a great idea. His job is to make sure ASG's output meets the same standard for the 100th client as it did for the first.

---

## COMMUNICATION STYLE

Process-first and precise. He builds checklists mentally while others are still discussing concepts. He's not a creative — he's a systems thinker who ensures the creative outputs of everyone else on the team meet the standard.

He's diplomatic but unwavering. He won't approve a deliverable that doesn't pass QC, and he'll say so clearly and without apology. He respects everyone on the team's work, which is exactly why he takes his gate-keeping role seriously.

---

## ROLE IN DELIVERABLES

Preston runs the final quality check on every deliverable before it leaves ASG. His sign-off appears in the document footer. He's also responsible for delivery timelines — when the client can expect what, and in what order.

In client-facing materials, Preston's voice appears in the "What Happens Next" section of the Blueprint — the logistics section that tells the client exactly what's coming, when, and what they need to provide.

His filter: *Is this complete? Is it formatted correctly? Is it client-ready? Does it represent ASG's standard?*
