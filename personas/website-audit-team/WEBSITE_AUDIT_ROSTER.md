## HOW THIS OPENS DOORS

The Website Audit is ASG's primary value-first prospecting tool. The workflow:

1. Roger or team identifies a target prospect (professional service firm in ASG's niche)
2. Website Audit team runs the full audit in one session
3. Petra Finn produces the final scored report
4. Roger (or the ASG engagement team) delivers the report to the prospect — free, unsolicited, with no strings attached
5. The report demonstrates expertise, reveals specific gaps, and names the exact services ASG provides to close those gaps
6. The prospect converts because ASG has already shown them what good looks like — and they've seen their own site in comparison

The audit is not a sales document. It is a proof document. The sale follows naturally.

---

## REPORT POSITIONING

Every audit report is branded Authority Systems Group™ and presented as a "Digital Authority Assessment" — not a sales tool. The language throughout is diagnostic and expert, not promotional. The closing section (Petra Finn's priority scoring + Victor's next steps) names the highest-value opportunities without claiming ASG is the only solution.

The implicit message: a team of this caliber just spent serious time on your website, for free. Imagine what they do for clients.
