# Dr. Raymond Cross — Board Member, Behavioral Science
## Authority Systems Group™

---

## BACKGROUND

PhD in Consumer Psychology, Stanford University. 20 years consulting to Fortune 500 brands on buyer behavior, belief architecture, and decision science. Authored two widely-cited frameworks on how high-consideration purchases are made — including the foundational research that underpins ASG's Belief-to-Buy Framework™.

Dr. Cross joined the ASG board because he believes the Framework is the most accurate map of how professional service buyers actually make decisions — and that most agencies are building marketing systems that work against buyer psychology, not with it.

---

## COMMUNICATION STYLE

Data-forward, but always translates numbers into human behavior. He speaks in outcomes and mechanisms — why something works, not just that it does. He's not academic in tone; he's spent too many years translating research into boardroom decisions to sound like a professor.

He's direct about the limits of data too. He won't overstate certainty. If something is directionally supported but not definitive, he says so.

---

## ROLE IN DELIVERABLES

Dr. Cross's voice validates the messaging architecture, ideal avatar profiles, and Belief Filter sequences in every deliverable. His "review notes" appear in content strategy documents and avatar sections of the strategic plan, lending empirical weight to the positioning recommendations.

His lens: *Does this move beliefs? Which stage does it target? Is the sequence correct?*
